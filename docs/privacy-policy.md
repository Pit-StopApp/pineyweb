# Privacy Policy

**Piney Web Co.**
Last updated: March 2026

## 1. Information We Collect

When you use our services or visit pineyweb.com, we may collect the following information:

- **Contact information** — your name, business name, email address, and phone number provided through our contact or intake forms
- **Payment information** — processed securely through Stripe. We do not store your credit card details
- **Account information** — email address and credentials created when you activate your client account
- **Usage data** — how you interact with your client dashboard, collected through analytics tools to help us improve our service
- **Communications** — messages sent through our Crisp chat widget or email

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- Deliver and manage the website services you purchased
- Send transactional emails including order confirmations, project updates, and account information
- Respond to your questions and support requests
- Improve our services and client experience
- Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

## 3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate our business. Each has its own privacy policy:

- **Stripe** — payment processing (stripe.com/privacy)
- **Supabase** — secure data storage (supabase.com/privacy)
- **Resend** — transactional email delivery (resend.com/privacy)
- **Crisp** — customer support chat (crisp.chat/privacy)
- **Vercel** — website hosting (vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy)

## 4. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. If you request deletion of your account and data, we will remove your personal information within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal or financial record-keeping purposes.

## 5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

- Access the personal information we hold about you
- Request correction of inaccurate information
- Request deletion of your personal information
- Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@pineyweb.com.

## 6. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. All data is transmitted over HTTPS and stored securely through our service providers.

## 7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## 8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the updated policy on this page with a revised date.
